# Module Spec: Enterprise Delivery And FAE Workflow

## Purpose

Teach customer-facing deployment as a system-design discipline.

## Learning Outcomes

- Convert customer workflow and site constraints into engineering requirements.
- Prepare site survey, deployment plan, rollback plan, acceptance criteria, handoff, and incident notes.
- Explain why FAE work is requirements translation, not only onsite installation.

## Required First Chapters

- `01-enterprise-ai-delivery-lifecycle.md`
- `02-requirement-interview-and-site-survey.md`
- `07-onsite-debugging-method.md`
