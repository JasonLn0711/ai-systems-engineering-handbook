# Module Spec: AI Systems Foundations

## Purpose

Explain what an AI system is and why enterprise AI requires model, data, infrastructure, workflow, governance, security, evaluation, and delivery to be designed together.

## Learning Outcomes

- Distinguish AI model, AI application, and AI system.
- Trace one AI request across product, data, model, policy, logging, and response layers.
- Explain why enterprise AI is a systems problem.

- `01-what-is-ai-systems-engineering.md`
- `02-model-vs-application-vs-system.md`
- `03-ai-request-lifecycle.md`
