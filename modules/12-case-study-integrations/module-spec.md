# Module Spec: Case Study Integrations

## Purpose

Connect modules into realistic enterprise AI scenarios.

## Learning Outcomes

- Map one enterprise AI scenario across product, data, model, infrastructure, governance, security, delivery, and evaluation.
- Explain trade-offs across modules.
- Produce case studies that support learning without exposing private source details.

## Required First Case Studies

- Enterprise AI Coach.
- Bank multi-agent governance.
- On-prem voice AI sales agent.
