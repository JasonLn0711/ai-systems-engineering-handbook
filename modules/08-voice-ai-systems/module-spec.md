# Module Spec: Voice AI Systems

## Purpose

Teach complete voice AI systems, including audio, VAD, ASR, diarization, TTS, wake word, real-time interaction, latency, and evaluation.

## Learning Outcomes

- Explain the voice AI pipeline from audio capture to playback.
- Design ASR, diarization, TTS, wake word, and latency evaluation workflows.
- Identify noisy audio, phone audio, overlap, hotword, and real-time failure modes.

## Required First Chapters

- `01-audio-signal-basics.md`
- `03-vad-and-speech-segmentation.md`
- `11-real-time-voice-agent-latency-budget.md`
