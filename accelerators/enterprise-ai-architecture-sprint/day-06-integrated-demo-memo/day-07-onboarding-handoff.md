# Day 7 Onboarding Handoff

Day 6 produces demo and memo evidence. Day 7 packages all sprint evidence into
an onboarding pack and first-30-days plan.

## Inputs From Day 6

- Demo script.
- Architecture memo.
- Latency table.
- Known limitations.
- Acceptance checklist.

## Day 7 Acceptance Criteria

- Evidence index links Day 1-6 artifacts.
- Onboarding pack explains the system in 10 minutes.
- First-week questions collect stack, deployment, model, data, eval, security,
  and milestone facts.
- First-30-days plan is split by week.
- Mock review checklist maps questions to artifacts.
