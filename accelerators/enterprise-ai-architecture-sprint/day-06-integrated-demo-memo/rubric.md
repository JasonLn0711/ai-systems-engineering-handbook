## Full-Credit Standards

| Category | Standard |
|---|---|

| Architecture memo | Connects audio, knowledge, agent, governance, deployment, and validation. |
| Latency evidence | Component p50/p95 plan or measurements. |
| Known limitations | Positive current scope, validation layer, future migration path. |
| Acceptance checklist | Functional, quality, latency, security, and ops evidence. |
| Source boundary | Synthetic and public-safe. |
