# Day 6 Demo / Memo Handoff

Day 5 produces deployment and capacity evidence. Day 6 consumes that evidence
to build an integrated demo script and architecture memo.

## Inputs From Day 5

- K8s manifest checklist.
- GPU sizing table.
- vLLM serving assumptions.
- p50/p95 validation plan.
- Deployment risk-control map.

## Day 6 Acceptance Criteria

- Demo script states hardware, model names, runtime, input condition, latency,
  and known limits.
- Architecture memo links audio, RAG, agent, governance, deployment, and
  validation layers.
- Known limitations use scope-control language.
- Acceptance checklist includes functional, quality, latency, security, and ops
  evidence.
