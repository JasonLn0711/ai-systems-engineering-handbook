# Source Boundary

This repository is allowed to be informed by private learning context, interviews, demo work, and project notes. It must not copy raw private material into the tutorial layer.

## Public-Safe

- generalized learning requirements
- general architecture patterns
- generic case studies without identifying private customer facts
- public standards and public documentation
- source-bounded summaries that do not expose private details

## Not Allowed

- raw interview transcript text
- salary, trial, offer, or negotiation details
- personal contact routes, private emails, LINE IDs, credentials, or tokens
- customer secrets or unpublished business claims
- private audio, screenshots, or identity-sensitive source files
- exact internal product claims that are not publicly verified

## Rewrite Rule

Convert source-derived insight into tutorial requirements.

Example:

```text
Private source says a company may need multiple agents over a shared SQL source.
```

Public-safe tutorial form:

```text
Enterprise AI systems often need shared knowledge or SQL access with project-specific skills, adapters, policy checks, and audit logs.
```
