# Review Rubric

Use this rubric before marking any chapter stable.

## Required Checks

- Key terms are defined before use.
- The chapter explains why the topic exists.
- The mechanism is clear enough to reason about failures.
- The workflow shows how engineers use the concept.
- Security and governance implications are explicit.
- Failure modes are concrete.
- Examples distinguish toy usage from production reality.
- Exercises or verification tasks are present.
- Cross-links point to related modules or glossary entries.
- Source boundaries are respected.

## Scoring

- `0`: missing or misleading.
- `1`: present but shallow.
- `2`: usable but needs review.
- `3`: stable and teachable.

A stable chapter should score at least `2` in every category and `3` in mechanism, workflow, security, and failure modes.
